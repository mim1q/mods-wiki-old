    Enemies of Mine Cells

    All of these enemies aim to be faithful representations of the monster of Dead Cells. The drops aren't very exciting right now, but will likely be improved upon as the mod's development progresses!

    Leaping Zombie

    The basic enemy of Mine Cells. Really common, attacks in melee range or by jumping at you.

    Spawns in

    • Overworld (portal structure)
    • Prisoners' Quarters
    • Promenade of The Condemned

    Drops

    • Rotten Flesh
    • Guts
    • Monster's Eye (rare)

    Grenadier

    Throws exploding bombs in your direction.

    Spawns in

    • Overworld (portal structure)
    • Prisoners' Quarters
    • Promenade of The Condemned

    Drops

    • Rotten Flesh
    • Guts
    • Monster's Eye (rare)

    Shieldbearer

    Charges their shield at you. Cannot be attacked from the front.

    Spawns in

    • Overworld (portal structure)
    • Prisoners' Quarters

    Drops

    • Rotten Flesh
    • Guts
    • Monster's Eye (rare)

    Undead Archer

    Shoots arrows. Pretty much like a regular Skeleton.

    Spawns in

    • Prisoners' Quarters

    Drops

    • Rotten Flesh
    • Guts
    • Monster's Eye (rare)

    Runner

    Performs a melee attack with a long windup that deals a ton of damage. Can teleport to you if you're far.

    Spawns in

    • Promenade of The Condemned

    Drops

    • Phaser (rare)

    Mutated Bat

    Has very little health but spawns in groups. Flies around you and dashes to attack.

    Spawns in

    • Promenade of The Condemned

    Protector

    Grants nearby hostile mobs the Protected status effect. Does not attack by itself.

    Spawns in

    • Promenade of The Condemned

    Inquisitor

    Shoots magic orbs that can phase through walls.

    Spawns in

    • Overworld (portal structure)

    Sewers' Tentacle

    Burrows under the ground and navigates toward you. Will unburrow to strike the player or perform a long-range charge if the path is clear.

    Spawns in

    • Insufferable Crypt
