    The Dimensions of Mine Cells

    Prisoners' Quarters

    A network of corridors linking the cells inhabited by what used to be the prisoners...

    Accessible from:

    • Overworld

    Leads to:

    • Promenade of The Condemned
    • Insufferable Crypt - requires Vine Rune

    Mobs:

    • Leaping Zombie
    • Grenadier
    • Shieldbearer
    • Undead Archer

    Promenade of The Condemned

    The yard of the Prison, with a really nice atmosphere

    Accessible from:

    • Prisoners' Quarters

    Leads to:

    None

    Mobs:

    • Leaping Zombie
    • Grenadier
    • Runner
    • Mutated Bat

    Insufferable Crypt

    Even the rats avoid this place...

    Accessible from:

    • Prisoners' Quarters - requires Vine Rune

    Leads to:

    • Overworld

    Mobs:

    • Conjunctivius - boss
    • Sewers' Tentacle
